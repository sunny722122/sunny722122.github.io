About

Ignited by my grandfather, a construction engineer, the blueprint he was working on took the most mysterious and grand memory of my early age. From that time on, I was fascinated by building things and solving problems. The delightful feeling when I accomplish even tiny project or resolve an issue is always so satisfying. I developed my career path to follow my passion. Shuttling in production lines or working on software that controls the exact workings of machines always makes me feel accomplished. There are definitely challenging moments, but I am always confident in my skills to work through them and find the correct solution. Previously, I worked on introducing data science and machine learning. This sparked my learning journey in data science, machine learning, and Python. After taking a software developer program I got experience on web developing, MVC, and I even learned Flutter app developing. What excites me most is what I learned during my time in the Instrumentation bridging course run by EMCN and NAIT. It helped me to bring together my past experience and new learning so that I could work on even more detailed projects. Learning is a nonstop journey, and I am looking forward to applying what I already know while continuing on that journey. Specialties: Industrial: PLC programming, HMI design, electrical diagram design, network configuration, instrumentation, Microcontroller programming Software development: Python, C++, C#, object-oriented programming, HTML, JavaScript, CSS, PHP, ASP.NET, MVC, Flutter, Git/GitHub Database: MySQL, MS SQL, PostgreSQL, Mango DB, SQLite

Beyond the world of code and systems, I write fiction that explores my new world. The intersection of technology and storytelling shapes how I see the world—each discipline informing the other, each demanding precision, creativity, and deep thought.
